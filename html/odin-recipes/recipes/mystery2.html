Mystery Recipe #2

Description

This recipe is rooted in this chef's love for delicious Southern ingredients, allowing the chef to combine local produce with exquisite taste.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 small red onion, cut into 1in-thick slices
  • 1 cup cooked butterbeans
  • Lady peas or Sea Island Red peas
  • 2 tomatoes seeded and diced 1/4in
  • 2 ears corn shucked, sans kernel, and boiled for 4 minutes
  • 1/2 small shallot, finely minced
  • 4 sprigs dill fronds, coarsely chopped
  • Several chives, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, plus more for drizzling if desired

Steps

  1. Prepare a hot grill or preheat broiler. Grill or broil onion slices, turning once, until lightly charred on both sides, about 3 to 4 minutes per side. Let cool, then cut into 1/2in dice.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the charred onion, butterbeans or file peas, tomatoes, corn, shallot, dill, and chives.
  3. Stir in sherry vinegar and season with salt and pepper. Stir in olive oil, taste, and adjust seasoning as needed.
